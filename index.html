Tom and Jerry

An American animated media franchise and run of comedic short films is called Tom and Jerry. It is one of the longest-running cartoon programmes and has been rated as one of the best of all time. Two short films featuring Jerry and Tom are featured in each episode.

Pokemon

Pokemon is a Japanese media franchise managed by The Pokémon Company, founded by Nintendo, Game Freak, and Creatures. It is set in the fictional Pokémon universe. At different locations within each region, the player can find different types .

Popeye

Popeye is a brazen, witty cartoon sailor who consumes a can of spinach because it is always handy and gains superhuman strength. Popeye has squinty eyes, protruding forearms, and a pipe in his mouth at all times. I only yam what I yam and nothing else.

Mr. Bean

Mr. Bean is immature, self-absorbed, extremely competitive and brings various abnormal schemes and contrivances to everyday tasks. He rarely speaks, it is normally a few mumbled words which are in a comically low-pitched voice.

Courage the Cowardly Dog

Courage the Cowardly Dog is an American animated comedy horror television series. Courage a kind yet easily frightened troublesome dog. He was abandoned as a puppy after his parents were sent into outer space by a crazed veterinarian.

Oswald

Each episode follows the daily experiences of an anthropomorphic blue octopus named Oswald accompanied by his beloved dog Weenie. This is a colorful world populated by anthropomorphic animals and mythological creatures.Oswald likes to play the piano.

Stuart Little

Stuart Little is an animated science-fiction adventure comedy-drama television series, based on the novel and films. The Animated Series teaches children how to solve their own problems and feel happy for their accomplishments.

Dragon Tales

Dragons Ord, Cassie, Zak, Wheezie, and the wise and sagelike Quetzal are among the creatures Emmy and Max's family encounters when they find the wonderful area known as Dragon Land. Dragons and children work together to overcome obstacles.
